Mission

        
Support location, coordination, and recovery of the crew of a downed Warthog aircraft over the Indian Ocean.

      

      

        
Situation

        
Aircraft status is unknown until confirmed. The first anchor points are last known position, time, weather, sea state, visibility, and comms status.

      

      

        
Task

        
Build the cleanest possible search picture from known facts, identify the datum, and call out gaps immediately.

      

      

        
Constraints

        
Time, fuel, weather, authority, jurisdiction, and communications all limit what can be done now.

      

      

        
Comms

        
Use short structured updates: what is known, what changed, what is missing, and what happens next.

      

      

        
Decision

        
Expand, narrow, or hold the search area based on grounded facts only.

What TRU must not do

        

          It must not invent coordinates, pretend the crew is recovered, or hide uncertainty. If the facts are missing, the honest answer is to name the gap and stop there.
